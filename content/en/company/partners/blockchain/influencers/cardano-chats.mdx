Cardano Chats is a podcast all about chatting with game-changers in the **Cardano** community.  
It is about getting new projects in the Cardano community out there for everyone to learn about and get involved in.  

The future of Cardano is bright and it will change the world. We are trying to help everyone enjoy the ride.  

</br>  

Cardano Chats will give **Cornucopias** a platform to reach the masses when needed as well as introduce projects that are similarly aligned and share the same goals.
