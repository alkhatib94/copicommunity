**Grow Your Stake (GYS)** is a **Cardano mission-driven stake pool**.  

They have partnered with **Drop4drop** and use **100% of their pool operator rewards** to fund **water wells** in places around the world that need it most.  

Their first well was completed recently in **Uganda**, bringing **clean water** to a village of about **400 people**.  

</br>

Cornucopias also plans to **delegate to GYS**, as the more delegation, the more pool rewards, which leads to more wells.
