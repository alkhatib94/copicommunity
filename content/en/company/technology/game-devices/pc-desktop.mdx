Cornucopias PC requirements:  

Cornucopias is designed to run well on a wide variety of PC hardware so that as many players as possible can play. To play Cornucopias at its best on PC, we recommend the following system specifications.  

<div className="note-box success transparent">
  DLSS was added in September 2025 to improve performance further  
  for compatible PCs using NVIDIA GPUs.
</div>  

</br>  

## Minimum System Requirements for Pre-Alpha Testing (January 2025)  

<ul className="list-disc pl-6 space-y-2">
  <li><b>GPU:</b> NVIDIA RTX 2080 (8GB) or NVIDIA RTX 3060 (8GB) or AMD RX 5700 XT (8GB)</li>
  <li><b>CPU:</b> Intel i5-10400k (6 core 2.9 GHz) or AMD Ryzen 5 3600 (6 core 3.6 GHz)</li>
  <li><b>RAM:</b> 16 GB DDR or greater</li>
  <li><b>OS:</b> Windows 10 or 11, 64-bit</li>
  <li><b>Storage:</b> 100 GB HDD</li>
</ul>  

</br>  

## Recommended System Requirements for Pre-Alpha Testing (August 2024)  

<ul className="list-disc pl-6 space-y-2">
  <li><b>GPU:</b> NVIDIA RTX 3080 (10GB) or NVIDIA RTX 4080 (16GB) or AMD RX 6800 (16GB) or greater</li>
  <li><b>CPU:</b> Intel i7-10700k (8 core 5.0 GHz) or AMD Ryzen 7 5800X (8 core 3.8 GHz) or greater</li>
  <li><b>RAM:</b> 32 GB DDR or greater</li>
  <li><b>OS:</b> Windows 11, 64-bit</li>
  <li><b>Storage:</b> 100 GB SSD</li>
</ul>  

</br>  

## Best System Requirements for Pre-Alpha Testing (February 2025)  

<ul className="list-disc pl-6 space-y-2">
  <li><b>GPU:</b> NVIDIA RTX 5080 (16GB) or NVIDIA RTX 5090 (32GB) or AMD RX 9070 XT (16GB) or greater</li>
  <li><b>CPU:</b> Intel i9-14900k (24 core 5.8 GHz) or AMD Ryzen 9 9900X (12 core 5.2 GHz) or greater</li>
  <li><b>RAM:</b> 64 GB DDR or greater</li>
  <li><b>OS:</b> Windows 11, 64-bit</li>
  <li><b>Storage:</b> 100 GB SSD</li>
</ul>  

<div className="note-box danger transparent">
  There is a Minimum Graphics Card (GPU) - Virtual RAM (VRAM) requirement of <b>8GB</b>  
  and a Minimum Memory (RAM) requirement of <b>16GB</b> to play Cornucopias.
</div>  

</br>  

## Other System Requirements for Pre-Alpha Testing (August 2024)  

It should be possible to run Cornucopias on other PC specifications than those listed above.  
However, limited testing will be done on older PCs due to the demand for graphics and multiplayer.  

<ul className="list-disc pl-6 space-y-2">
  <li><b>GPU:</b> NVIDIA GTX 1070 (8GB) or AMD RX 5600XT (6GB) or greater</li>
  <li><b>CPU:</b> Intel i7-4770k (4 core 3.5 GHz) or AMD Ryzen 7 2700 (8 core 3.2 GHz) or greater</li>
  <li><b>RAM:</b> 16 GB DDR or greater</li>
  <li><b>OS:</b> Windows 10 or Windows 11, 64-bit</li>
  <li><b>Storage:</b> 100 GB HDD</li>
</ul>  

</br>  

## Graphic Card Settings and Scaling options - DLSS & FSR  

In 2025 **NVIDIA DLSS** and **AMD FSR** were integrated into the Cornucopias Game and these technologies improve gameplay for all compatible NVIDIA and AMD graphics cards.  

For the latest release schedule, see the [Roadmap](/game/about-the-game/road-map).
