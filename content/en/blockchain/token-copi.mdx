## Token History  

All **3,840,000,000 COPI tokens** were originally minted on **Ethereum (ERC-20)** and then immediately **locked and ported** (minted) on the **Binance BNB Smart Chain (BEP20)**.  

The choice to move them to BNB was to enable **lower transaction fees** for token owners while maintaining flexibility on Ethereum for the future.  

- **24th March 2022:** 25,000,000 COPI Tokens on the BNB Chain were **burnt** and then **minted** on the Cardano Network as Native Tokens.  
- **24th November 2023:** Liquidity was added to **Uniswap**, effectively re-launching COPI on Ethereum.  

</br>

## Fixed Supply  

The COPI token has a **fixed supply** and is native to its original mint as an ERC-20 token.  
To maintain this fixed supply across chains, the token is **Locked/Unlocked** on Ethereum and **Burned/Minted** on other chains.  
This process is managed by **ChainPort** and is fully audited.
