# Valkyrie F9-R  

A **"built-for-racing" NFT** launched on **1st November 2024**, part of the **Flying Vehicle Series**.  

The **2024 F9-R by Valkyrie** is engineered for high-speed racing, featuring a sleek, aerodynamic design and twin jet engines powered by advanced Gen-Core technology.  
It was the first **S-Class** vehicle introduced to Cornucopias.  

Built for precision and performance, it delivers rapid acceleration and top-tier handling, ensuring smooth control even at high speeds.  
The streamlined frame minimizes drag for maximum speed, making the Valkyrie F9-R a top choice for racers who demand both performance and agility on the track.  

<div className="note-box success">
  The Valkyrie F9-R is also a flying vehicle that is used to travel from the <b>Sector Hub</b> to the <b>Themed Zones</b> or the <b>City</b>.  
  It can also be used for racing and is a <b>Racing Class S</b> vehicle.
</div>

## Utility  

The Valkyrie F9-R has the following utility within Cornucopias:  

<ul className="styled-list">
  <li>It is a flying vehicle and is used to travel from the Sector Hub to the Themed Zones or the City.</li>
  <li>It can be used for racing in a Mega Dome such as Calido Valley.</li>
  <li>You will be able to rent your NFT to other players to play or race with.</li>
  <li>You will be able to stake your NFT.</li>
  <li>It looks great in the racing lobby and you will be able to show it off to other racers when they are waiting to race in the same lobby as you.</li>
  <li>As an NFT, it will form part of your Cornucopias NFT collection.</li>
  <li>You own the NFT asset and will be able to sell or trade it.</li>
</ul>  

<div className="note-box">
  This was the first in-game asset mint to be held on both the <b>Cardano</b> and <b>Base</b> Chains to expand the player base to multiple blockchains.
</div>
