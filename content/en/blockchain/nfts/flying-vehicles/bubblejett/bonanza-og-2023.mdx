# Bubblejett Bonanza OG Custom 2023

An exclusive NFT launched on **6th July 2023** and given **FREE** to OG Role Holders of the Cornucopias Discord, part of the **Flying Vehicle Series**.

The **Bubblejett Bonanza OG Custom 2023** is a flying vehicle used to travel from the **Sector Hub** to the **Themed Zones** or the **City**. It can also be used for racing and is a **Racing Class A** vehicle.

The OG Edition was created for early supporters who joined the Cornucopias Discord and was given to them for **FREE** (a small transaction fee to mint and send was required; this was less than 1 ADA).

The **OG role** is awarded to community members who help others, are respectful and kind, and have genuine conversations in chat.
