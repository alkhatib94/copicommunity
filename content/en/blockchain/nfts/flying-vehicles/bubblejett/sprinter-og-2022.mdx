# Bubblejett Sprinter OG Custom 2022  

An exclusive NFT launched on **11th April 2022** and given **FREE** to OG Role holders of the Cornucopias Discord - part of the **Flying Vehicle Series**.  

The **Bubblejett Sprinter OG Custom 2022** is a flying vehicle used to travel from the **Sector Hub** to the **Themed Zones** or the **City**.  
It can also be used for racing and is a **Racing Class B** vehicle.
