# Bubblejett Sprinter 2022  

The **First Public NFT** from Cornucopias launched on **4th March 2022**, part of the **Flying Vehicle Series**.  

The **Bubblejett Sprinter** is a flying vehicle used to travel from the **Sector Hub** to the **Themed Zones** or the **City**.  
It can also be used for racing and is a **Racing Class C** vehicle.

## Utility  

The Bubblejett Sprinter 2022 has the following utility within Cornucopias:  

<ul className="styled-list">
  <li>It is a flying vehicle used to travel from the Sector Hub to the Themed Zones or the City.</li>
  <li>It can be used for racing in a Mega Dome such as Calido Valley.</li>
  <li>You will be able to rent your NFT to other players to play or race with.</li>
  <li>You will be able to stake your NFT.</li>
  <li>It looks great in your garage and you can show it off to your friends when they visit your home bubble.</li>
  <li>As an NFT, it will form part of your Cornucopias NFT collection.</li>
  <li>You own the NFT asset and will be able to sell or trade it.</li>
</ul>
