The NFT2Tree has the following utility within Cornucopias:  

<ul className="styled-list">
  <li>One or more real-life trees will be planted around the world when the NFT is purchased and for ongoing secondary market sales.</li>
  <li>Additional benefits to NFT2Tree holders will be announced in the future.</li>
  <li>As an NFT, it will form part of your Cornucopias NFT collection.</li>
