# Zero G - Stratos Solace Explorer Special Edition Jetpack  

The Zero G - Stratos Solace Explorer Special Edition Jetpack launched on 9th November 2024.  

The Solace Explorer Limited Edition Pack builds on the Zero G Stratos Pack with enhanced power, perfect for explorers demanding extended flight time and faster recharge.  

Engineered with an improved quantum compression system, this pack enables longer, uninterrupted flights, allowing users to traverse greater distances with ease.  

The advanced recharge rate further minimizes downtime, so the pack is ready for action sooner, keeping you constantly on the move.  

Reliable, efficient, and powerful, the Solace Explorer Pack is designed for adventurers aiming to cover more ground in less time, pushing the boundaries of exploration.
