# Rando's Metalworks - Junkerjet Jetpack  

The Rando's Metalworks - Junkerjet Jetpack launched on 1st November 2024.  

Introducing the JunkerJet by Rando's Metalworks - where one man's scrap becomes another man's lifeline. Built from salvaged parts, this rugged jetpack is perfect for explorers who see the value in the discarded.  

Powered by two refurbished engines, it compresses air through quantum chambers to lift you off the ground with ease.  

Tough, gritty, and made to survive, the Junker Jetpack turns what others throw away into your greatest asset. **Rando's Metalworks: Turning junk into treasure for fearless adventurers.**

## Utility  

The Junkerjet Jetpack has the following utility within Cornucopias:  

<ul className="styled-list">
  <li>It is a Jetpack and is used to fly around the various Themed Zones and Megadomes.</li>
  <li>It is also a small Backpack and can extend the amount of inventory items you can carry around Cornucopias.</li>
  <li>It has to be equipped to your Avatar to enable its use in-game.</li>
  <li>As an NFT, it will form part of your Cornucopias NFT collection.</li>
  <li>You own the NFT asset and will be able to sell or trade it.</li>
