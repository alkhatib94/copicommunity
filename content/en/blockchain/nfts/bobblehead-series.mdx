# NFT - Bobblehead Series  



The Bobblehead series of NFTs are a fun addition to the Cornucopias NFT set.  

They can be placed on some of the flying vehicles that have a large dashboard, or within your **Home Bubble**.  

This was the first NFT sale for Cornucopias that used the **$COPI token** as a form of payment and marked a major leap forward in its utility.  

<div className="note-box">
  <strong>Snapshot:</strong> ADA (₳) = $0.3123 and $COPI = $0.01773 @ 1500UTC on 12th December 2022 - the price was <b>not</b> used in calculating the NFTs in ADA (₳) or $COPI.
</div>

## Utility  

The Bobbleheads have the following utility within Cornucopias:  

<ul className="styled-list">
  <li>Ornamental item for dashboards of flying vehicles (e.g. Bubblejett Sprinter).</li>
  <li>Decoration for tables inside your Sector Hub Home.</li>
  <li>Acts as a Tier 1 access pass to future Bobblehead mints.</li>
  <li>Part of your Cornucopias NFT collection.</li>
  <li>Fully tradable and sellable NFT asset.</li>
</ul>
