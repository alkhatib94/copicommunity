## Utility  

The Racing Suit will have the following utility within Cornucopias:  

<ul className="styled-list">
  <li>It is an item of clothing that can be worn by your character in-game whilst racing.</li>
  <li>Access to Cheeky Crypto discord and partner is given along with discount off Cheeky Crypto merchandise for the NFT holders.</li>
  <li>There will be exclusive races for Cheeky Racing Suit NFT holders.</li>
  <li>May offer better racing performance due to streamlined racing suit (yet to be decided).</li>
  <li>As an NFT, it will form part of your Cornucopias NFT collection.</li>
  <li>You own the NFT asset and will be able to sell or trade it.</li>
</ul>
