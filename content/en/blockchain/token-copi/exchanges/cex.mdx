# CEX - Centralized Exchange  

Centralized exchanges are platforms that allow users to buy and sell cryptocurrencies for fiat currencies such as the US dollar or digital assets like BTC or the Cornucopias $COPI token.  

They operate as trustworthy brokers in deals and frequently serve as custodians, keeping and safeguarding your funds.

## Pros of Centralized Exchange  

<ul className="styled-list">
  <li>User-friendly interface and simple platforms</li>
  <li>An added layer of security and trustworthiness</li>
</ul>  

<br />

## Cons of Centralized Exchange  

<ul className="styled-list">
  <li>Vulnerable to hacking or cybersecurity threats</li>
  <li>Charges service fees</li>
  <li>Controlled by a centralized entity - can be shut down, which results in your currency being locked</li>
</ul>
