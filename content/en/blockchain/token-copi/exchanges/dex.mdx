# DEX - Decentralized Exchange  

## Blockchain Dependant Decentralized Exchanges  

A decentralized exchange (or **DEX**) is a peer-to-peer marketplace where transactions occur directly between crypto traders.  
DEXs fulfill one of crypto’s core promises: fostering financial transactions that aren’t officiated by banks, brokers, or any other intermediary.

## Pros of Decentralized Exchange  

<ul className="styled-list">
  <li>Completely private and anonymous</li>
  <li>No need to transfer assets to a third party</li>
  <li>Fees are redistributed to liquidity providers, not to a centralized entity</li>
</ul>  

<br />

## Cons of Decentralized Exchange  

<ul className="styled-list">
  <li>Trading in fiat currency is not permitted</li>
  <li>Risk of impermanent loss for liquidity providers</li>
  <li>Liquidity can be thin, making it harder to execute large orders</li>
</ul>
