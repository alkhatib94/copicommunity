# Rock

Rock is an essential crafting resource in Cornucopias. This resource can be used to craft buildings within Cornucopias.

<div className="note-box success">
  <strong>Note:</strong> The life cycle begins with rock found in quarry's.
</div>

## Rock

Rock is a naturally occurring solid material acquired after mining a material point using a pickaxe. Commonly found in quarries or rock faces scattered across themed zones. Rock will have different characteristics to make them identifiable to the player. 

Rocks can be acquired as solid rock. This must be taken to the brickworks to be processed into construction bricks. 

## Bricks

Bricks are a crafting resource essential for building. Extracted as rock, ground into shale, then cooked in a kiln.
