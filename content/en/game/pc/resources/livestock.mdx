# Livestock

This refers to the animals in the game players can harvest for resources. These are passive beings which can be found in themed zones or raised on farms. 

<div className="note-box success">
  <strong>Note:</strong> This resource can be used to craft meals, essential to player sustenance.
</div>

## Cows

A cow is a common passive animal and can be cultivated in themed zones.  
They are a source of leather, beef, milk and butter.  

## Chickens

A chicken is a common passive animal and can be cultivated in themed zones.  
They are a source of chicken, feathers and eggs. 

## Sheep

A sheep is a common passive animal and can be found roaming wild across the themed zones.  
They are a source of raw wool.
