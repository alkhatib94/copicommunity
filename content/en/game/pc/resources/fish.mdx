# Fish

Fish are passive beings found in oceans and rivers.  
They are caught by fishing and can be cooked as part of a meal.

<div className="note-box success">
  <strong>Note:</strong> The life cycle begins with catching fish from rivers and oceans using a fishing rod.  
  The fish is then taken to a kitchen to be prepared and cooked.
</div>

## Ocean Fish  

Found along the coastlines of themed zones.  
Players must venture out on boats into deep water to capture them.  

## River Fish  

Found in the rivers of the themed zones.  
Players must spot the fish and stand on river banks to get their catch.
