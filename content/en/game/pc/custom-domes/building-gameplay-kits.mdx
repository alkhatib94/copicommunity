# Custom Dome - Building Kits & Gameplay Kits

Creating your Custom Dome will be fun with our Drag-and-Drop building kits. You will be able to place each object from a choice of many building kits.

Mix and match your Custom Dome -why not have a Pop Concert inside an Art Gallery while at the same time having a player vs player capture the flag going on?
