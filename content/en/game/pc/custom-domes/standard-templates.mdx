# Custom Dome - Standard Templates

Users who purchase a Custom Dome Land NFT can choose from a selection of standard templates.

<div className="note-box danger">
  <strong>Note:</strong> Not all templates will be playable when Custom Domes are released and are subject to change.
  Over the months and years, some templates will be enhanced and new ones added.
</div>

The following templates are planned with more to come in the future:

- Project/Company HQ   
- Nightclub experience   
- Theater / stage experience   
- Festival experience   
- NFT Art Gallery experience   
- Global Expo events   
- Business Meeting   
- Educational Center   
- Player vs Player (PVP) Games   

<div className="note-box success">
  Many more templates will be added as the game evolves.
</div>
