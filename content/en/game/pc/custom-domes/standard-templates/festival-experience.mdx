# Custom Dome Template - Festival Experience

Template for a Player owned Custom Domes that can be customized

A player-owned custom dome will have the option to be turned into a festival venue, complete with multiple stages and activity areas. Custom domes have the capacity to allow for many simultaneous players, through the use of Size Your Event technology. 

The entire location can be customized by yourself or by utilizing the Community Built Asset Marketplace. This space can be curated to suit a replica of the muddy Glastonbury experience or Coachella desert. 

With the use of a Green Screen or a Custom Avatar with Motion Capture, the artist could virtually come alive on stage. A live stream or recorded set could be being projected at the same time on multiple 200ft screens.
