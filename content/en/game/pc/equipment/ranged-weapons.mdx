# Ranged Weapons  

Ranged weapons are a form of equipment and are the most effective way of dealing damage at a distance to creatures and livestock in Cornucopias. They come in the form of bows and arrows. Players can craft them by using resources gathered from themed zones.  

<div className="note-box">
  Unique weapons can be purchased as NFTs.
</div>

## Bows  

Bows are ranged weapons used during ranged combat.
