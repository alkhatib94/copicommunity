# Melee Weapons

Weapons are a type of equipment in Cornucopias. Weapons are the main way of defeating enemies by dealing damage to them directly. Weapons have a set amount of durability and will break when this value reaches zero. 

<div className="note-box">
  Unique weapons can be purchased as NFTs.
</div>

## One-handed Swords  

One-handed swords are mid damage melee weapons, wielded with one hand, which can be used in tandem with shields.  

## Two-handed Swords  

Two-handed swords are a high damage weapon, wielded with two-hands for high effect. 

## Shields  

Shields are items used for blocking in melee combat.
