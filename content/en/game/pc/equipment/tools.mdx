# Tools   

A player needs tools in order to gather resources from the themed zones. These resources can then be sold or processed in preparation for crafting.  

<div className="note-box">
  <strong>Note:</strong> Unique tools can be purchased as NFTs.
</div>

## Axes   

Axes are a form of equipment used to chop down trees.  

## Pickaxe   

Pickaxes are a form of equipment used to hack away at ore and rock.  

## Hoes  

Hoes are a form of equipment used to harvest crops and plants.
