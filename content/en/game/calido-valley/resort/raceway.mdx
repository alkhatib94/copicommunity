# Calido Valley Raceway

The **Calido Valley Raceway** can be found in the **Calido Valley Mega Dome**.

</br>


This is the area you get to via the **Calido Valley Resort** to go racing around the tracks in the invigorating and fast-paced **Cornucopias Racing League**.
