# Calido City

**Calido City** can be found in the **Calido Valley Mega Dome** on the *Outer Rim* of Cornucopias.

</br>

This is the area in front of the racing tracks where players can **meet, go shopping, explore attractions**, and when ready enter the **Calido Valley Raceway** to race around the tracks in the invigorating and fast-paced **Cornucopias Racing League**.

<figure>
  <img src="/images/themed-zones/calido-valley/calido-city/main.avif" alt="Calido City in the Calido Valley Mega Dome" />
  <figcaption>Calido City doubled the size of the explorable map of the Calido Valley Megadome</figcaption>
</figure>

</br>

Calido City was introduced to Calido Valley in the **“City Slickers” release** in **December 2024**.  

</br>

Found within Calido City is the first **Aero Trails course**  a purpose-built, fast-paced timed mini-game where players fly through rings located across the City.  
Various **fastest time competitions** have been held where winners are rewarded with **$COPI**.  

</br>

The **Nexus Express** allows players to trade in their **In-Game Credits** for various in-game items such as clothes and food.

</br>

## Features

<ul className="styled-list">
  <li><strong>Calido City:</strong> See the other side of Calido for the first time. Still a work-in-progress and will be updated over time to support many game features.</li>
  <li><strong>Aero Trails Game Mechanic:</strong> A jetpack flying course through rings in Calido City that starts near the airport entrance and takes you to great heights. Compete with others for the best time. <em>Jetpack required.</em></li>
  <li><strong>Early Crowd and NPC System:</strong> Bringing Calido City to life.</li>
  <li><strong>Calido International Airport:</strong> Can be partially visited and is still WIP.</li>
  <li><strong>Early Banking Functionality:</strong> Collect credit chips and deposit them into your account! Various ATM machines are available around Calido City.</li>
  <li><strong>Nexus Express:</strong> Available in Calido City.</li>
</ul>
