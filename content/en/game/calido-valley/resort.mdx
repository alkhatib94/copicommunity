# Calido Valley Resort

The Calido Valley Resort can be found in the **Calido Valley Mega Dome** on the *Outer Rim* of Cornucopias.

This is the area in front of the racing tracks where players can **meet, go shopping, explore attractions**, and when ready enter the **Calido Valley Raceway** to race around the tracks in the invigorating and fast-paced **Cornucopias Racing League**.
