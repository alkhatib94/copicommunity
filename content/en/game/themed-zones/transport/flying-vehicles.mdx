# Flying Vehicles

The most advanced form of personal travel across the Cornucopias metaverse is by flying vehicle.  
Store them in the garage at the **Sector Hub** and use them to explore the metaverse and travel to the many **Themed Zones** and **Custom Domes**.

Also, travel to and compete in races within the **Calido Valley Mega Dome**.

<div className="note-box">
  Flying Vehicles can be purchased as NFTs.
</div>
