# Public Transport

Public transport is a **service** players use to connect them to the Cornucopias Metaverse.  
This is a **flying bus** which arrives at regular intervals and links the **Sector Hubs**, **Themed Zones**, and **The City**.

<br />

The City has its own type of public transport -the **City Bus**.
