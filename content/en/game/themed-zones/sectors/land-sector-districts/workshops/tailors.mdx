# Tailors

The workstation necessary for crafting clothing.  
Players must bring resources to begin the tailoring process.

<br />

Tailors stations can be found in every district and include a **loom** and a **tailors table**.

<br />

## Loom

This is a machine which allows players to convert thread into processed fabric.

<br />


## Tailors Table

This is the main interface where players combine their processed fabrics to craft clothing and armor.
