# Kitchen

The workstation necessary for crafting **meals**.  
Players bring crops to begin the cooking process.

<br />

Cooking stations can be found in every district.

<br />

## Chopping Board

The workstation used to chop **fruit, vegetables, and meat**.

<br />

## Hobs

The workstation used to **fry** or **boil** crops.

<br />

## Oven

The workstation used to **bake** ingredients.
