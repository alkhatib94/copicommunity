# Tannery

Tanning is the process of treating **skins and hides** to produce crafting materials such as **leather** and **suede**.

<br />

## Tanning Rack

The workstation which allows players to convert **rawhide** into **leather**.

<br />

## Leather Table

The workstation players use to process **leathers** into **items**.
