# Blacksmith

The workstation necessary for crafting **tools** and **weapons**.  
Players combine resources at the forge to begin the crafting process.

<br />

Blacksmith stations can be found in every district and include a **forge** and **smelting furnace**.

<br />

## Armor Table

The workstation which enables players to combine resources to craft **armor**.

<br />

## Weapons Table

The workstation which enables players to combine resources to craft **weapons** and **tools**.

<br />

## Engineers Table

The workstation which enables players to combine resources to craft **metal construction elements**.
