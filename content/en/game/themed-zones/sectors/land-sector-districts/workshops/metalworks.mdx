# Metalworks

The workshop necessary for processing **ore** into **metal concentrate**.

<br />

Metalworks can be found in every district and include a **grinder**, **shaking table**, and **smelting furnace**.

<br />

## Grinding

The workstation used to remove waste rock from ore and separate valuable minerals.

<br />

## Smelting Furnace

An industrial oven used to heat ore in order to convert it into **metal concentrate**.
