# Episode 104 - <span className="text-orange-500">Timber Creek & Purple Track</span>  

In COPICafe Episode 104, Rob and Josh share their experiences in **Timber Creek**, updates on **resource gathering**, and showcase **community-made maps and tools**.  

</br>  

Special guest **Scruffy** joins to introduce the new **purple track**, a unique driving experience between the red and yellow tracks.  

</br>  

The team also provides updates on **land plots, Esperanza, file nodes**, and other improvements while teasing a **new leak** at the end of the episode.  

</br>  

[00:00:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=JXWw-ifMCRU&t=0) Rob and Josh open by sharing community images and running around in **Solace 2**.  

</br>  

[00:05:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=JXWw-ifMCRU&t=300) Journey to **Timber Creek**. The hosts reflect on its potential for development and praise guild-created **in-game maps** for their accuracy and creativity.  

</br>  

[00:10:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=JXWw-ifMCRU&t=600) Discussion of **logging areas** and resource gathering. Players can only cut trees in designated areas to prevent reckless logging. Insights on graphics performance between laptops and high-end setups.  

</br>  

[00:15:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=JXWw-ifMCRU&t=900) Showcasing **community contributions**:  
- Corn Mafia Guild’s spinning wheel for day/night cycles.  
- Knights Guild’s interactive map with districts, fishing spots, and monorail routes.  
These creations inspire and add depth to the game.  

</br>  

[00:20:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=JXWw-ifMCRU&t=1200) Updates on **land plot adjustments** and new resources. Developers plan to release official plot data for public use. Showcase of AI-generated community designs: robot dog, robot eagle, and jetpack hero.  

</br>  

[00:25:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=JXWw-ifMCRU&t=1500) Introduction of **Scruffy** and the **purple track**. Positioned between red and yellow tracks in difficulty, it includes unique features like **cloud driving and inverted perspective**. Longer than the yellow track, it emphasizes strategy over speed. Release expected shortly after the **NFT sale**.  

</br>  

[00:30:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=JXWw-ifMCRU&t=1800) Updates from Scruffy:  
- Adjustments to the **yellow track** for smoother racing.  
- Progress on **Esperanza**.  
- Testing of **updated file nodes** to reduce server costs.  
- Work on ensuring **land plots are clear and level**.  

</br>  

[00:35:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=JXWw-ifMCRU&t=2100) Future plans for **Solace**. Shared resources exist across servers but may update differently. Mining areas will include **specific minerals** with zone-based variations.  

</br>  

[00:40:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=JXWw-ifMCRU&t=2400) The hosts tease this week’s **leak**, closing with excitement for upcoming content and community engagement.
