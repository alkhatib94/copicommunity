# Episode 101 - <span className="text-orange-500">Next Stop... Rare Evo</span>  

In COPICafe Episode 101, Rob and Josh discuss their experiences at **Rare Evo** and share their excitement about community interactions and industry partnerships.  

</br>  

They introduce the **new build of Cornucopias**, which includes the launch of **Solace 1, 2, and 3**.  

</br>  

Updates include the upcoming **interactive land grab website**, environmental adjustments, new **lighting and points of interest**, removal of GPU restrictions, and **DLSS support** for Nvidia cards.  

</br>  

The hosts also introduce **Eric Peterson (“Wingman”)** as an advisor and reflect on **community-driven development** in Web3 compared to traditional closed-door publishing.  

</br>  

[00:00:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=oLGzbM_mx2w&t=0) Rare Evo Day 2 at Caesars Palace. The team discusses the success of the event, meeting in person, and heartwarming community moments such as meeting **14-year-old Duke**, a Holocache leaderboard player.  

</br>  

[00:05:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=oLGzbM_mx2w&t=300) Community reactions to playing the **new Solace build**.  

</br>  

[00:10:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=oLGzbM_mx2w&t=600) Booth offerings and clarification that Cornucopias is branded as a **life simulator with MMO qualities**. Introduction of **Philip Black** (game economist, Amazon New World) and **Jennifer Donahoe** (League of Legends marketing expert) to the advisory board.  

</br>  

[00:15:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=oLGzbM_mx2w&t=900) Announcement of **Eric Peterson (“Wingman”)**, creator of **Wing Commander**, joining the advisory board. Updates on **Solace 1-3**, world travel system, and land grab with custom markers.  

</br>  

[00:20:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=oLGzbM_mx2w&t=1200) Milestone: launch of the **three giant worlds of Solace**, larger than GTA V. Discussion of the **World Travel Loop** (dome-to-dome, cloud gate). Upcoming features: **tree chopping, mining, and MMO mechanics**.  

</br>  

[00:25:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=oLGzbM_mx2w&t=1500) Farid praises the team for creating a world larger than **Skyrim and GTA V combined**. QA team highlighted for bug-fixing. Upcoming: **racing tournaments** and the **Valkyrie F9-R Flying Vehicle sale on Base**.  

</br>  

[00:30:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=oLGzbM_mx2w&t=1800) Rob and Josh reflect on the **100th episode milestone**, community growth, and Web3 openness compared to traditional publishing. Closing montage of Solace with soundtrack **“Butterflies” by 7even**.
