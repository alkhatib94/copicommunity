# Episode 109 - <span className="text-orange-500">A Glimpse of Fear</span>  

In Episode 109 of COPICafe the hosts Rob and Josh discuss the progress of *Cornucopias,* which offers players a vast open world with unique environments and communities.  

</br>  

They also share their excitement about upcoming features, such as new vehicles, decorations, and the Nexus system. The creators discuss improvements to the game, including new premium outfits, graphical enhancements, and animal AI.  

</br>  

They introduce the concept of material cards and functional backpacks, and discuss strategies for combat. Additionally, they discuss new items, such as the Corn Cuties Backpacks and the Quantum Jack Helmet, and share their experiences wearing masks during the recording. Despite some challenges, they maintain a positive and engaging conversation throughout the episode.
