# Episode 98 - <span className="text-orange-500">DEcentralized GAming</span>  

In COPICafe Episode 98 titled *"DEcentralized GAming"*, the hosts Josh Jones and Rob Greig reflect on their **three-year journey** building Cornucopias and its community.  

</br>  

They highlight the importance of **registering nodes**, the upcoming release with **Avatar updates, leaderboards, and land plot selection tools**, and their partnership with **DEGA**, a decentralized gaming platform.  

</br>  

The episode also features **community Q&A**, discussions about transportation within the game, and plans for future **racing tournaments** with big prizes.  

</br>  

[00:00:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=uud9aFv8CP8&t=0) Josh reflects on three years of Cornucopias’ development and community building. They preview upcoming content: **nodes, a new release, community questions, and a partner guest**.  

</br>  

[00:05:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=uud9aFv8CP8&t=300) Josh talks about **fly fishing** before transitioning to project updates. **Node NFTs** have been delivered, and players can now register file nodes for July rewards. Registrations must be completed by **July 31st**, and nodes can be linked across chains and wallets.  

</br>  

[00:10:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=uud9aFv8CP8&t=600) Rob shares that around **45% of nodes are registered**. He stresses the importance of registering for rewards. Upcoming release includes **Avatar updates, leaderboards, and land plot selection tool**. Work continues on scaling infrastructure with Amazon to handle tens of thousands of players.  

</br>  

[00:15:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=uud9aFv8CP8&t=900) Community Q&A session. Travel within cities will use **public transportation**, but NFTs like **GTi Javelin, Bubblejett Bonanza, and Astro 4** will allow faster zone travel. Mounts will be best for dome travel. The team considers monthly Q&A formats like Twitter Spaces or Discord AMAs.  

</br>  

[00:20:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=uud9aFv8CP8&t=1200) Guest **Carlos Rene (CEO of DEGA)** explains DEGA’s mission: empowering creators to design, publish, and monetize games cheaply. DEGA specializes in **2D pixel art games for low-cost smartphones** and supports Cornucopias with **BASE Chain NFT integration**. They are also working on **racing chain data** publishing.  

</br>  

[00:25:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=uud9aFv8CP8&t=1500) Discussion on moving **racing data** to production. Partnership with **Infinity Gaming** (Latin America’s major eSports company) to broaden Web3 adoption. Plans for a collaboration strategy announcement next week.  

</br>  

[00:30:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=uud9aFv8CP8&t=1800) Announcements and initiatives for **DEGA**. Community encouraged to join **DEGA’s Discord and Telegram** for updates.  

</br>  

[00:35:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=uud9aFv8CP8&t=2100) Josh and Rob announce a **racing tournament** with qualifiers, weekly leaderboards, and a final bracket round. Influencers may join, and prizes are planned. They remind viewers that the game is still **pre-alpha** but encourage participation and fun. Gratitude is expressed to the community.
