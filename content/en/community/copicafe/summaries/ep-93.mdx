# Episode 93 - <span className="text-orange-500">Consensus 2024 Special</span>  

In COPICafe Episode 93 titled *"Consensus 2024 Special"*, the hosts Josh Jones and Rob Greig welcome the **Corn Community** and share highlights from the second day of the Consensus conference.  

</br>  

The team showcases their **interactive Cornucopias booth**, featuring multiple screens that attracted large crowds and sparked numerous questions from attendees. They emphasize the importance of professionalism, cross-chain strategies, and being part of the **DePIN trend**.  

</br>  

Key topics include the **rollout plan for decentralized file nodes**, the introduction of **leaderboards** as a reward system, and customer testimonials. Special guest **Tobias**, senior environment artist, shares insights into digital water creation and his excitement about attending the event in person after 1.5 years with the team.
