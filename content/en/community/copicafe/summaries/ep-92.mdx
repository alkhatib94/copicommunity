# Episode 92 - <span className="text-orange-500">Taking the Lead</span>  

In COPICafe Episode 92 titled *"Taking the Lead"*, the hosts Josh Jones and Rob Greig discuss the advancements in their open world MMO project, **Cornucopias**.  

</br>  

The team announced changes to the **SPO Stake pool bonus** from 105% to 115%, aiming to build up the pool further and contribute to the Cardano network.  

</br>  

They also introduced a new **delegate subscription for the file nodes system**, allowing those without technical abilities to have nodes run for them by public pool operators.  

</br>  

Excitement builds for the upcoming **Consensus conference**, and the team plans to attend and network with other projects.  

</br>  

The team also discusses their roles, recent work on **Solace**, and improvements to the **land distribution system**. They express excitement about the upcoming **land release** and the capabilities of the **leaderboard system** for competitions and tournaments.  

</br>  

[00:00:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=N7lt060Y9mk&t=0) Updates on the SPO Stake pool offering. Bonus for staking COPI tokens changed from 105% to 115%. Pool has a saturation of 16M Ada with nine blocks already made. Goal: decentralize Cardano further.  

</br>  

[00:05:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=N7lt060Y9mk&t=300) Node system update: non-technical users can delegate to public pool operators. Hosts need 8 nodes minimum, earning 2 delegates per node. Aim: more decentralization with commercial-grade software.  

</br>  

[00:10:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=N7lt060Y9mk&t=600) Discussion of curated vs. procedurally generated land. Solace land hand-sculpted for realism and uniqueness. New character artist joins to accelerate animal creation. Focus: making land feel alive.  

</br>  

[00:15:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=N7lt060Y9mk&t=900) Excitement for Consensus conference (14K+ attendees). Plans: content creation, interviews, networking with projects. Reflections on project progress and new hires. Light banter about AI travel agency.  

</br>  

[00:20:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=N7lt060Y9mk&t=1200) Scruffy discusses streaming backgrounds, marketing, and QA roles. Updates on Solace efficiency, bug fixes, and small improvements. Focus: refining processes and better user experience.  

</br>  

[00:25:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=N7lt060Y9mk&t=1500) Scruffy impressed by the land distribution system. Solace 2 tested and Solace 3 coming. Equal chance for land plots, fair system. Tools for testing/exploration still improving.  

</br>  

[00:30:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=N7lt060Y9mk&t=1800) Excitement for land release. Scruffy prefers resource-based land selection. Visual comparisons between Solace 1, 2, and 3. Discussion of the size and scope of areas.  

</br>  

[00:35:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=N7lt060Y9mk&t=2100) Bug-fixing progress discussed. Aim: prioritize critical bugs, though perfection impossible. Team reiterates commitment to Cardano chain. Mention of possible kid-friendly racing game in future.  

</br>  

[00:40:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=N7lt060Y9mk&t=2400) Leaderboard system: competitions and tournaments for racing, fishing, and more. Racing rewards coming. Mention of Valkyrie F9-R sale. Hints at seasonal events and esports potential. Closing thanks and sign-off.
