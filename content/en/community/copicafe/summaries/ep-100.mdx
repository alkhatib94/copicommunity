# Episode 100 - <span className="text-orange-500">RareEvo Centenary Special</span>  

In this **Special Edition** of COPICafe Episode 100 titled *"RareEvo Centenary Special"*, **Farid of dApp Central** hosts a panel with **Josh Jones and Rob Greig** live from **Caesars Palace Conference Centre, Las Vegas**.  

</br>  

They share stories of community connections, clarify that Cornucopias is now positioned as a **life simulator with MMO-like qualities**, and introduce a **new advisory board** including **Philip Black (game economist), Jennifer Donahoe (marketing), and Eric Peterson (game design veteran)**.  

</br>  

The team also discusses the addition of the three giant worlds of **Solace**, expresses gratitude to their QA team and community, and reflects on their **100th episode milestone**.  

</br>  

[00:00:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=oLGzbM_mx2w&t=0) Rare Evo Day 2 at Caesars Palace. The team shares drinks, stories, and the excitement of meeting in person. Heartwarming stories include **14-year-old Duke**, a Holocache leaderboard player, meeting the team.  

</br>  

[00:05:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=oLGzbM_mx2w&t=300) Community reactions to playing the new **Solace build** are shared, showcasing excitement and positive feedback.  

</br>  

[00:10:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=oLGzbM_mx2w&t=600) Clarification that Cornucopias is now branded as a **life simulator with MMO-like qualities**. Introduction of new advisors: **Philip Black** (Amazon Games, New World) and **Jennifer Donahoe** (League of Legends).  

</br>  

[00:15:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=oLGzbM_mx2w&t=900) Announcement of **Eric Peterson**, creator of **Wing Commander**, joining the advisory board. Discussion of Solace 1-3, the new **world travel system**, and the **land grab feature** with maps and markers.  

</br>  

[00:20:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=oLGzbM_mx2w&t=1200) Introduction of the **three giant worlds of Solace**, part of the first themed zone of twelve planned. The map is larger than GTA V. Features include dome-to-dome exploration and cloud gate travel. Upcoming features: **tree chopping, mining, and MMO mechanics**.  

</br>  

[00:25:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=oLGzbM_mx2w&t=1500) Farid thanks the team for creating a world larger than **Skyrim and GTA V combined**. QA team praised for bug-fixing. Upcoming events: **racing tournaments** and **Valkyrie F9-R Flying Vehicle sale on Base**.  

</br>  

[00:30:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=oLGzbM_mx2w&t=1800) Rob and Josh reflect on 100 episodes, community growth, and Cornucopias’ place in the Cardano ecosystem. Closing montage of **Solace** with soundtrack **“Butterflies” by 7even**.
