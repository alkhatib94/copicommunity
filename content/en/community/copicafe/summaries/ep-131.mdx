
# Episode 131 - Coming of Age  

### Summary  

Cornucopia is a ground-breaking open-world MMO set in a futuristic sky-world composed of floating islands, each encased in advanced domes and featuring unique environments and communities. Players can create personalized avatars, explore vast landscapes, and engage in diverse activities such as crafting, combat, commerce, and racing.  

</br>

In this latest update, co-CEOs Rob Greig and Josh Jones share exciting news about the upcoming game release scheduled for the week following the July 4th holiday, highlighting key features such as Avatar 3.0, a new movement system, combat mechanics, and various cosmetic additions.  

</br>

A significant portion of the update focuses on the evolving node infrastructure, explaining the transition from a reward system heavily weighted on base rewards to one centered primarily on node performance. The team also teases the long-anticipated land grab feature, the apartment builder, and a marketplace upgrade to support multi-item crypto purchases.  

</br>

Rob and Josh reflect on the remarkable progress made over four years, emphasizing the game’s technical optimization, high frame rates, and readiness for a marketable alpha launch soon.  

</br>
