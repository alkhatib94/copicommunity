# Episode 111 - <span className="text-orange-500">Size Matters</span>  

In Episode 111 of COPICafe the hosts Rob and Josh discuss the latest goings on in the land of *Cornucopias.*  

</br>  

They provide updates on the exciting phase three sale featuring a significant cargo ship, the Juggernaut, and the recent successful early playtests that engaged the community with new challenges and jetpacks.  

</br>  

As they discuss ongoing content development and enhanced marketing strategies, the team emphasizes building strong connections with the Cardano Foundation for ecosystem growth while planning a major marketing push coinciding with the game's anticipated alpha release.  

</br>  

Highlights include the exploration of visually stunning environments, innovative travel techniques, and intricate gameplay features essential for player immersion, supplemented by community feedback.  

</br>  

The conversation concludes with insights on future updates, a focus on vehicle travel using player NFTs, and a sneak peek of new features in development, showcasing the team's commitment to enhancing the player experience.
