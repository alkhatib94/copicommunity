# Episode 106 - <span className="text-orange-500">Limited Edition</span>  

In Episode 106 of COPICafe the hosts Rob and Josh discuss various updates with *Cornucopias.*  

</br>  

The upcoming sale will include jetpacks, backpacks, vehicles, and apparel, with pricing details not yet finalized. The sale will not release all items immediately but in phases.  

</br>  

The team also discusses upcoming hats, including the **Burger Barn Deluxe** and **Quantum Jack Avatar** hats.  

</br>  

They mention updates on nodes, node reward claiming, and jetpack testing. The team plans to involve the community this month and is working on defining performance metrics and implementing security features.
