# Episode 102 - <span className="text-orange-500">Enter the Multiverse</span>  

In COPICafe Episode 102 titled *"Enter the Multiverse"*, Rob and Josh, Co-CEOs of **Cornucopias**, showcase the **World Travel Loop**, updates on **Solace exploration**, and the upcoming **node infrastructure release**.  

</br>  

They also compare **Unreal Engine 5.0 vs 5.3**, announce a **100,000 COPI competition**, and reveal plans to list the game on the **Epic Store**.  

</br>  

[00:00:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=prDdb7ZJNPs&t=0) Rob and Josh introduce the open world MMO and share their drinks (banana milkshake and water). They outline topics: **World Travel Loop, node updates, Unreal Engine comparison, and tournaments**.  

</br>  

[00:05:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=prDdb7ZJNPs&t=300) Showcase of the **World Travel System** with massive sectors and hubs. Solace images feature **deer, caves, and hidden spots**. Apartments and advertisement boards highlight hub life.  

</br>  

[00:10:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=prDdb7ZJNPs&t=600) Hidden discoveries in **Solace 2**, including **caves, waterfalls, and an electronics factory** for crafting. A **sunset screenshot contest** is announced. Sneak peek of a **general store** in development.  

</br>  

[00:15:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=prDdb7ZJNPs&t=900) Discussion of game size: **three Solace worlds (8x8 km each)** plus hubs and Calido Valley. All handcrafted. Node system integration with launcher underway. Preparations for **community-hosted nodes** via Docker.  

</br>  

[00:20:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=prDdb7ZJNPs&t=1200) Upcoming **node infrastructure release**. Rewards tied to uptime and quality. Public/private pools possible, with delegation. Target: full rollout by end of year. Comparison of **UE5.0 vs UE5.3** shows improved saturation and assets.  

</br>  

[00:25:00](https://youtube.com/watch?v=prDdb7ZJNPs&t=1500) Reflections on development progress from prototype to **Pre-Alpha**. Announcement of **100,000 COPI competition** across several weeks. Preview of the **Art Book** with Solace mining and log processing machines.
