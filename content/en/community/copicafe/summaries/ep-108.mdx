# Episode 108 - <span className="text-orange-500">Quantum Jen</span>  

In Episode 108 of COPICafe the hosts Rob and Josh discuss the upcoming sale in *Cornucopias.*  

</br>  

The developers are refining the node backend systems and plan to invite testers soon, with a public release by the end of the year.  

</br>  

The team announces the sale of assets on both the Cardano and BASE chains. The sale, which starts on Wednesday, 23rd October 2024, is the third version of the Marketplace and includes seasonal assets for Halloween.  

</br>  

A free NFT helmet named **Quantum Jack Helmet** will be given away during the sale. The team also discusses changes to the accessibility of *Cornucopias,* allowing new Web2 players to pay for early access with a credit card and Game Pass.  

</br>  

The next build release, **Quantum Autumn,** includes seasonal assets and the free NFT helmet.  

</br>  

Special guest **Jennifer Donahoe,** who has just joined the Cornucopias board, shares her background in the toy industry and her transition into the video game industry, emphasizing the importance of authentic engagement and decentralized marketplaces for players. She discusses new ways to engage communities through technology, specifically mentioning Windwalk's gamified platform. The team also answers viewer questions and provides information on jetpack models.
