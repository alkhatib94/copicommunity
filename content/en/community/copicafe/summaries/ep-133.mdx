# Episode 133 - Land Unlocked  

### Summary  

In episode 133 of COPI Café, Rob Greig and Josh Jones provide a comprehensive update on Cornucopias, covering recent hot fixes, the upcoming land grab event, avatar contest, phase three sale details, and the premium apartment sale. The team discusses recent performance improvements after community feedback, especially related to VRAM optimization and multiplayer experience. They introduce an avatar contest featuring their advanced Avatar 3.0 system and reveal details about the upcoming phase three sale, which includes free and paid assets like the Superverse weapon.  

</br>

The apartment sale is also previewed, emphasizing customization and asset creation with in-house development. The centerpiece of the episode is the detailed explanation of the land grab event: its process, timing, and strategic considerations for claiming land plots using NFTs. The team also discusses future plans for the marketplace, secondary sales of land and developed properties, and upcoming features such as crafting, mining, and building ownership. Lastly, they touch on planned node pool adjustments and community engagement efforts, including interviews with influencers.
