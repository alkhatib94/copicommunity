# Episode 105 - <span className="text-orange-500">Purple Track & NFT Sale</span>  

In COPICafe Episode 105, Rob and Josh discuss **new NFT sales, the purple racing track, marketplace updates, and onboarding improvements**.  

</br>

</br>
