# Episode 135 - Elevated Spaces  

### Summary  

The episode delivers a comprehensive update on Cornucopias, highlighting apartment customization, the phase three sale, land grab progress, node ecosystem growth, and community events. Rob Greig and Josh Jones give a transparent overview in COPI Café Episode 135, with an immersive tour of Solace Hub City’s “plus tier” apartments, showcasing extensive customization options. Key updates include the launch of the first partnership-branded Superverse weapon NFT, marketplace improvements, land grab testing, and details on node rewards. The community avatar contest and an upcoming Las Vegas event are also spotlighted, underscoring Cornucopias’ focus on creativity, ownership, and engagement.
