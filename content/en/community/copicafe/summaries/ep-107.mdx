# Episode 107 - <span className="text-orange-500">The Game Economist</span>  

In Episode 107 of COPICafe the hosts Rob and Josh discuss the upcoming sale in *Cornucopias.*  

</br>  

The sale includes new assets, some of which are rare and others free, like the **Quantum Jack Helmet.**  

</br>  

The speakers discuss the game's features, such as jetpacks, nodes, and marketing strategies.  

</br>  

They welcome a guest, **Philip Black,** who has contributed to their tokenomics and in-game economy.  

</br>  

The conversation also includes answering viewer questions and discussing the game's infrastructure and player experiences.
