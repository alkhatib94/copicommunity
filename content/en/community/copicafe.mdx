# COPICafe  

COPICafe is a regular show created for the main Cornucopias YouTube channel, hosted by the two [Cornucopias Co-CEOs Rob Greig and Josh Jones.](/the-company/founders)

</br>

The first episode ‌aired on Saturday 26th March 2022 and has been positively received ever since.

</br>

In COPICafe, Rob and Josh discuss the latest events from within the Cornucopias Game. 

</br>

<div className="note-box">
  <strong>Fun fact:</strong> There were two other pilot episodes created. Things moved so fast in Cornucopias, 
  that by the time both were ready to be published the content was already out of date.
</div>
