# COPILeaks  

**COPILeaks** is set up to share snapshots of work-in-progress and aims to keep the community excited and up-to-date with game development activity.  

</br>  

Images and videos are shared on all Cornucopias Social Media Channels and the Discord Channel.  

</br>
