# COPIWiki  

**COPIWiki** was created to share the inner and ongoing work of **Cornucopias**.  
It is a **living document**, which means it will be updated regularly.  

The **COPIWiki** was launched on **19th May 2022** and the initial version contained **186 pages** of information.  

The COPIWiki has its own **Product Owner** and took over 6 months to plan, build, validate and update.

## Some of the key thoughts behind creating the COPIWiki:  

<ul className="styled-list">
  <li>Be used as an easy entry point to the game for newcomers</li>
  <li>Be used with onboarding of new staff</li>
  <li>Be a source of truth directly from the team that is building the game</li>
  <li>Be a living document that evolves over time</li>
  <li>Be the perfect place for influencers and other content creators to gather information without barriers</li>
  <li>Be accessible on all devices</li>
  <li>Show status updates on the project builds</li>
  <li>Show more than just gameplay, but an entire overview of the company</li>
  <li>Provide factual information alongside gameplay such as Blockchain NFT Policy IDs</li>
  <li>Have a living history, warts-and-all of the builds and decisions we make</li>
  <li>Show the 7-year roadmap in all its glory</li>
  <li>Be the most detailed Whitepaper ever built</li>
</ul>
