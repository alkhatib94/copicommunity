# June 2023  

## SUMMARY  

We’re at the end of a very ambitious Q2 for Cornucopias, with big events and milestones just around the corner in Q3. Highlights include:  

<ul className="styled-list">
  <li>Smooth implementation of <b>Agile Planning</b> to additional areas of the business - development teams becoming less siloed and more collaborative as we head towards release.</li>
  <li>New <b>Player Portal website</b> was launched and the Player Roles were introduced - thousands of people have already registered their profile name.</li>
  <li>Game and Launcher API was created and is being tested.</li>
  <li>Announced a new <b>Layer 2 with DEGA</b> to hold leaderboard and other data from the Cornucopias Racing League.</li>
  <li>Our team grew by 2 this month, with more to come in Q3/Q4.</li>
  <li>We integrated <b>AI into the COPIWiki</b> - see Episode 56 of COPICafe for more information.</li>
  <li>Launched the new YouTube series <b>Cornucopias Universe</b> with Lore Master Jake as the new face - this show will expand over time, so get in on the ground floor!</li>
  <li>Planning a free <b>OG NFT mint</b> for Q3 - stay tuned for details.</li>
  <li>Internal testing for the Racing Game and Calido Valley Resort, with QA preparing for public testing.</li>
  <li>Our Online Community grew significantly following this month’s major gameplay leaks. See community info below.</li>
</ul>  

As we close out Q2, we’ll be updating our global roadmap. Stand by for more info from the top!
