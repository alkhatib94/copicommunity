# Consensus 2022  

In June 2022 we took **7 members** of Cornucopias to the annual **Consensus event** in Austin, Texas.  

</br>

## Highlights  

<ul className="styled-list">
  <li>
    Charles Hoskinson (CEO of IOHK) mentioned Cornucopias by name in his Cardano keynote at the pre-Consensus Cardano special evening (night before Consensus).
  </li>
  <li>
    Charles Hoskinson mentioned Cornucopias by name in a <b>Yahoo Finance</b> interview.
  </li>
  <li>
    Josh &amp; Rob delivered a 30-minute Cornucopias presentation where they:
    <ul className="styled-list">
      <li>Showed a UE5 graphics showcase video of the upcoming game.</li>
      <li>Presented the <b>Desktop Launcher</b> with hints of future functionality.</li>
      <li>Rob Greig demoed the <b>Home Bubble Building System</b>.</li>
      <li>Josh Jones &amp; Josh Miller (IOHK) played an early eSports demo of the GTi Javelin racing game.</li>
      <li>Discussed the <b>tree-planting initiative</b> with Veritree.</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li>Met with Cardano influencers including BigPey &amp; Kaizen Crypto.</li>
  <li>Spoke with Cornucopias supporters and NFT holders.</li>
  <li>
    Hosted a mini <b>Javelin racing competition</b> - won by Damon of Charli3 (time: 1:46s).  
    Notable participant: Tamara Haasen (President of IOHK).
  </li>
  <li>Handed out exclusive Cornucopias T-Shirts.</li>
  <li>Attended multiple Cardano &amp; Community evening events.</li>
  <li>Hosted several private business networking lunches.</li>
  <li>Engaged with Cardano projects regarding future collaborations/partnerships.</li>
  <li>Spoke with the <b>Cardano Foundation</b> about future initiatives.</li>
  <li>Discussed Cornucopias community projects with <b>Project Catalyst</b> (not for funding).</li>
</ul>
