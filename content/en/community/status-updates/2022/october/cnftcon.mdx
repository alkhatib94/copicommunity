In October 2022 we took **11 team members** and **1 Discord moderator** from Cornucopias to the annual **cNFTCon** in Las Vegas, Nevada.  
The event had **140 booths** showcasing Cardano ecosystem projects and was attended by **3,500+ people** across the 2-day weekend.

</br>

<figure className="img-center">
  <img src="/images/status/2022-10/hoskinson-selfie.avif" alt="Charles Hoskinson with Cornucopias team" />
  <figcaption>Charles Hoskinson (CEO of IOG) stopped by for a selfie with the Cornucopias team.</figcaption>
</figure>

Cornucopias had one of the **largest 30x30 ft booths** with:
- 5 sofas, 3 chairs, 6 stools  
- A backstage area with large Cornucopias divider  
- 5 plants + 4 tables  
- 3x 55" TVs + 1x 75" TV  

These were used to showcase **2 playable Metaverse demos**.

## Highlights

- **Solace Demo:** 10-minute challenge to find *Farmer Joe* in Solace-1.  
  Day 1: 2 winners - Day 2: 9 winners. Each successful player will receive a **special backpack NFT**.  
- **First Ever Player:** 9-year-old Ryan.  
- **First to Find Joe:** Farid (dApp Central YouTuber).

## Racing Demo

A **3-player multiplayer racing demo** (first previewed at Consensus 2022) was updated and playable:  
- Rebuilt fully in **Unreal Engine 5**  
- Upgraded graphics + assets  
- Multiplayer via local network  
- API with auto leaderboard  
- Vehicles: GTi Javelins, Bubblejett Sprinters, +2 new exclusive cars
