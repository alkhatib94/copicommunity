# Cardano Summit 2022

**Lausanne - Switzerland - Saturday November 19th to 21st**

<p>
Each year the Cardano Foundation holds a community event to show off the blockchain, share milestones, and tease upcoming features. It was a 3-day event in Lausanne, Switzerland. There was also a <b>secret virtual garden</b> that remote attendees could visit to search and collect coins - when all coins were collected a commemorative Cardano Summit NFT was sent to their Cardano wallet.
</p>
