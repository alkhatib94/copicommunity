## Definitions

<div className="grid md:grid-cols-2 gap-4">
  <details className="rounded-xl border border-white/10 bg-zinc-900/60 p-4 open:bg-zinc-900/80 transition">
    <summary className="cursor-pointer text-lg font-semibold">FPS - Frames per second</summary>
    <div className="mt-3 text-sm leading-relaxed">
      This determines how smooth your gameplay looks and feels. Higher FPS equals smoother play.
      Lower settings often boost FPS, but they can reduce visual quality-balance is key.
    </div>
  </details>

  <details className="rounded-xl border border-white/10 bg-zinc-900/60 p-4 open:bg-zinc-900/80 transition">
    <summary className="cursor-pointer text-lg font-semibold">VRAM - Virtual RAM</summary>
    <div className="mt-3 text-sm leading-relaxed">
      That’s your GPU’s memory, and it’s shown in the bottom-right corner of the settings menu.
      It’s an estimate and may not always be exact.
      <div className="mt-2 rounded-lg border border-white/10 bg-zinc-800/60 p-3">
        <b>Tip:</b> Press <kbd>Windows</kbd> + <kbd>G</kbd> for the Windows Game Bar to track FPS/VRAM in real time,
        or use third-party tools like <i>MSI Afterburner</i> for more detail.
      </div>
    </div>
  </details>
</div>
